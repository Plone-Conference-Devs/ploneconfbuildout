Join us for

Plone Conference 2010

October 27th - October 29th 2010
Thistle Grand Hotel, Bristol, UK

Hosted by Netsight Internet Solutions

Key Dates

  • Oct 25th-26th
    Training
  • Oct 26th
    Showcase
  • Oct 27th-29th
    Main Conference
  • Oct 30th-31st
    Developer Sprints

Attendees can expect three full days of talks from both experienced Plone developers and those new to Plone, including an Open Space / Unconference day on the third day.

Aiming to promote Plone to the wider CMS / WCM community, this year's conference will also feature a related one-day event aimed at analysts and decision makers on the 26th October. This mini-conference will showcase a number of case studies from successful Plone projects and the future roadmap for Plone.

And if that's not enough, we will also have two days of training and workshops before the conference for those new to Plone, or wanting to brush up on their skills. After the conference there will be two days of developer sprints where you will get the chance to work alongside some of the best brains in the community.

Of course, it wouldn't be a proper Plone gathering without a bit of a party! This year's conference party will be held at a fantastic city centre venue, where we will be hosting a beer & pie festival showcasing some of Bristol's tastiest local produce.

More details regarding the conference will be posted on the site soon, including registration rates, accommodation and travel. In the mean time more information can be found on plone.org.
